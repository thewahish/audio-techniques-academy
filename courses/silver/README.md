### Course Overview
Building on Bronze level foundations, this intermediate course covers professional recording techniques, advanced mixing concepts, and audio post-production workflows.

- Master advanced recording techniques
- Understand professional mixing workflows
- Learn audio post-production processes
- Equipment selection and optimization
- Introduction to mastering concepts

### Prerequisites
- Completion of Bronze Level or equivalent experience
- Basic understanding of audio concepts
- Access to a digital audio workstation
