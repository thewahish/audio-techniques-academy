### Course Overview
This foundational course introduces students to the essential concepts of audio engineering and recording techniques. Perfect for beginners with no prior audio experience.

### Learning Objectives
- Understand basic audio concepts (frequency, amplitude, phase)
- Learn fundamental recording techniques
- Familiarize with basic audio equipment
- Introduction to digital audio workstations
- Basic mixing and level balancing

### Course Content
- **audio-fundamentals-bronze.html** - Main course presentation

### Prerequisites
- No prior audio experience required
- Basic computer literacy
- Willingness to learn

### Duration
Approximately 8-10 hours of content

### Next Steps
Upon completion, students are ready for the Silver Level - Intermediate Audio Techniques.
